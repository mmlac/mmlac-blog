StreamWallets is a loyalty system for Twitch streamers. It allows them to define rewards for their users to earn and increase loyalty by offering a wallet system to hold these rewards in. The rewards can be redeemed via a chatbot and viewed on the streamwallets.com website. Detailed analytics are available for streamers, as well as tools to improve their stream; Examples include a spinning wheel for rewards as well as a popup notification for embedding in OBS and similar streaming software.
